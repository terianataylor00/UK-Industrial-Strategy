Research & Publications

Core Themes

Our work focuses on five strategic pillars:

  1. Innovation & Technology — Strengthening R&D and commercialisation capacity.
  2. Skills & Talent — Ensuring the UK workforce is equipped for the industries of tomorrow.
  3. Infrastructure & Investment — Building resilient systems to support sustainable growth.
  4. Net Zero & the Just Transition — Balancing economic competitiveness with environmental responsibility.
  5. Global Competitiveness — Positioning the UK as a leader in key international markets.

Research Approach

We combine insights from Winmark's C-Suite networks, primary research and policy expertise to produce reports, roundtables, and recommendations that are both strategically grounded and operationally relevant.

Our aim is to deliver measurable benefits—creating a stronger economy, a fairer society, and a more sustainable environment.
