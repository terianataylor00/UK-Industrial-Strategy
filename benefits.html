Benefits

The UK Industrial Strategy is a Winmark-led initiative, supported by key UK and foreign businesses. Its benefits extends across the economy, society, and environment:

Wealth & Economics

  • Drives the collective impact of a £22.2 trillion economy, unlocking greater industrial innovation, collaboration, and personal wealth.
  • Delivers a more successful UK economy that is stable, growing, and globally competitive, with increased tax inflows and business confidence.

People and Society

  • Attracts both local and national investment, levelling up regions and increaing productivity.
  • Creates better jobs and career opportunities, leading to more fulfilled, skilled, and resilient people.

Home & Environment

  • Improves the utilsation of limited resources such as land and energy.
  • Enables quick wins and reduces waste, resource depletion, and environmental impact.
  • Makes the UK a better place to live for everyone, balancing prosperity with sustainability.
