About Us

Our Purpose

The UK Industrial Strategy Think Tank was established to create a platform for a practical collaboration between leaders in business, academia, and government.

We aim to deliver clear insights, credible evidence, and strategic recommendations, that strengthen the UK's industrial base and accelerate innovation-led growth.

Why This Matters

The UK stands at a pivotal moment. Global competition, rapid technological change, and the transition to net zero demand a new model of economic leadership.

Our work ensures the UK remains competitive, sustainable, and future-ready—equipped to thrive in a changing global economy.

Our Funders

The think tank is mainly funded by Winmark, the C-Suite membership organisation that connects leaders from over 16 professional networks, including CEOs, CFOs, CIOs, CMOs, CHROs, and CLOs.

Under CEO John Jeffcock, Winmark belives that business has a vital role to play in shaping national strategy. Through this initiative, Winmark and its members being together expertise from across industries to drive evidence-based, actionable recommendations.
