UK Industrial Strategy

A Think Tank Powered by Winmark

The UK Industrial Strategy think tank explores how the United Kingdom can build a more competitive, sustainable, and resilient economy.

Our work focuses on how business, government, and academia can collaborate to drive productivity, innovation, and inclusive growth.

This initiative is led by Winmark and supported by key UK and international businesses. Funded primarily by Winmark, whose CEO, John Jeffcock, leads a global network of C-Suite executives, the think tank brings together leaders from across sectors to shape strategies that deliver long-term economic success.
